2️⃣ AI BEHAVIOR INSIDE COMMUNITIES (CRITICAL DIFFERENTIATOR)
🎯 Core Principle

AI should assist, not dominate

A. Where AI appears
On every new post (auto-summary)
On demand (“Explain this”)
In private chat (deep dive)
B. AI RESPONSE STRUCTURE (STRICT FORMAT)

Every AI output MUST follow:

1. What this could be (simple explanation)
2. Risk level (Low / Medium / High)
3. What you can do now
4. When to see a doctor
5. Disclaimer
C. Behavior Rules
✅ MUST DO
Simplify medical jargon
Ask follow-up questions (max 2)
Highlight red flags clearly
Stay neutral (no panic, no overconfidence)
❌ MUST NOT DO
No definitive diagnosis
No medication prescriptions
No contradiction of doctors
No long paragraphs
D. Smart AI Features (High Value)
1. Duplicate Detection

If same question exists:

“Similar discussions found” → show threads

2. Thread Summarization

Long thread → AI gives:

Key takeaways
Most agreed answers
3. Escalation Trigger

If high-risk keywords:

Chest pain
Suicidal thoughts
Severe bleeding

👉 AI forces:

“Seek immediate care” + action buttons
E. Tone Calibration
Mental health → empathetic
Chronic disease → guiding
Fitness → motivating
